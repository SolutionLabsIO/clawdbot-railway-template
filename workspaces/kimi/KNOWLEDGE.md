# Scholistico — Domain Knowledge

## About Scholistico

Scholistico is an online education platform specializing in holistic wellness and alternative medicine professional certification courses. The platform serves professionals seeking to add holistic health credentials to their practice.

## Business Model

- **Product**: Self-paced online certification courses
- **Platform**: WooCommerce storefront
- **Project management**: Monday.com
- **Team communication**: Slack
- **Automation**: n8n workflows

## Course Categories

Scholistico courses cover holistic wellness disciplines including:
- Holistic nutrition and dietetics
- Aromatherapy and essential oils
- Herbalism and plant medicine
- Energy healing modalities
- Mind-body wellness practices
- Professional certification preparation

## Student Profile

- Working professionals adding holistic credentials
- Career changers entering wellness industry
- Existing wellness practitioners expanding scope
- International student base (multilingual considerations)

## Content Standards

- All health claims must be evidence-informed
- No medical advice or diagnostic claims
- Citations required for clinical/scientific references
- Encouraging tone — students are investing in their growth
- Accessible language — avoid unnecessary jargon
- Inclusive and culturally sensitive

## Key Workflows

### New Course Launch
1. Content development (modules, assessments, resources)
2. Monday.com tracking (content pipeline board)
3. WooCommerce product setup (pricing, descriptions, images)
4. Marketing materials (email sequences, blog posts, social)
5. Student onboarding flow

### Student Support
1. Check WooCommerce order status
2. Verify enrollment and access
3. Answer course content questions
4. Escalate technical issues to Clawdio
5. Process completion certificates

### Content Pipeline
1. Topic research and outline
2. Draft creation (Kimi can help)
3. Review and fact-checking
4. Publication and distribution
5. Performance tracking

## Important Notes

- Student data is confidential — never share outside support contexts
- Refunds follow WooCommerce store policy — check before processing
- Course pricing changes require Charles' approval
- New course topics should align with existing certification tracks
