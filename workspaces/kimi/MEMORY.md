## Content Standards

- All health claims must be evidence-informed — cite sources
- Never make medical claims or give medical/diagnostic advice
- Encouraging, accessible tone — students are investing in their growth
- Avoid unnecessary jargon — professional but approachable
- Inclusive and culturally sensitive language
- Blog posts: 800-1500 words, SEO-friendly, actionable takeaways
- Course content: clear, structured, progressive difficulty
